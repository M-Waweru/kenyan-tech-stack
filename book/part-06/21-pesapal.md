# 21. Pesapal: The SME Checkout Layer

## Lead

For an SME, payment failure is not a metric. It is lost rent, lost stock, and lost customer trust. A stable checkout flow can be the difference between growth and collapse.

The rail is SME-friendly digital acceptance and settlement. Builders include onboarding teams, merchant support desks, and risk controls designed for small businesses. Users include retailers, clinics, schools, restaurants, and online sellers. Referees include consumer-protection rules and financial compliance requirements.

Behavior changed as SMEs adopted digital channels where payment completion and settlement predictability improved. Online and offline commerce began to converge around the same rails.

The shadow includes support costs, fraud attempts, and margin pressure in competitive gateway markets.

Bridge: once merchants and institutions digitized payments at scale, government workflow digitization accelerated.
