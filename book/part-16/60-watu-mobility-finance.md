# 60. Watu and Mobility Asset Finance

## Lead

For a boda rider, financed ownership can transform earning potential and autonomy. Daily repayment discipline becomes part of work routine.

The rail is mobility asset lending tied to digital collection systems. Builders include lenders, dealers, and risk operations. Users include riders and transport households. Referees include credit regulation, transport policy, and dispute resolution mechanisms.

Behavior changed through increased ownership pathways where traditional credit access was constrained.

The shadow is conflict around enforcement, repossession, and fraud attempts.

Bridge: electrification introduced new standards and interoperability questions.
