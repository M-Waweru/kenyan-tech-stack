## Deep-Dive Analysis

Part 16 focuses on systems people use when failure is expensive: crops, power, mobility, and shock response. The connective tissue is financing and coordination under constraint. Builders package assets and advice into rails, users optimize survival and upside, and referees (courts, regulators, donors, standards bodies) decide whether scaling remains fair.
