## Deep-Dive Analysis

This part tracks how Kenyan communities convert social trust into financial throughput, then progressively instrument that trust with digital evidence. The builder-user-referee triangle is explicit: builders encode local norms into ledgers and workflows, users negotiate urgency and accountability in real time, and referees (law, regulators, and group constitutions) define when informal coordination becomes formal obligation.
