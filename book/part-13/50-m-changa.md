# 50. M-Changa: Harambee Goes Link-Based

## Lead

When crisis hits, speed matters. A fundraiser link can circulate across families, alumni groups, churches, and diaspora networks within hours. Contributions that once required physical coordination now arrive in streams of small digital commitments.

The rail is digital social fundraising. Builders include payment-enabled crowdfunding platforms and trust-verification processes. Users include households and communities mobilizing emergency support. Referees include platform safeguards, fraud monitoring, and legal compliance around collections.

Behavior shifted from local physical fundraising events to distributed, rapid-response digital mobilization.

The shadow is verification burden: urgency can be exploited by scammers if checks are weak.

Bridge: WhatsApp remained the dominant coordination channel across all these flows.
