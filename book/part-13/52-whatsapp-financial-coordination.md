# 52. WhatsApp as Financial Coordination Layer

## Lead

A single chat thread can host reminders, receipts, voting, conflict resolution, and payment escalation. In practice, many community financial systems run on conversational governance.

The rail is chat-mediated financial coordination. Builders include group admins and platform workflow practices adapted by communities themselves. Users include members managing obligations in real time. Referees include social legitimacy, platform moderation limits, and evidence standards when disputes arise.

Behavior changed by reducing coordination costs and maintaining group momentum despite geographic dispersion.

The shadow is narrative fragility. Rumors and misunderstandings can escalate quickly in text-only spaces.

Bridge: diaspora flows and digital-dollar alternatives increasingly fed these same social-finance channels.
