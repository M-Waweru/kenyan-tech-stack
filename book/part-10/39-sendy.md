# 39. Sendy: Logistics Dreams and Friction

## Lead

**August 2023**, **TechCrunch** reported **Sendy**—a **Kenyan** **logistics** startup that helped retailers source **FMCGs** from manufacturers, among other services—was shutting down and exploring an asset sale. Co-founder **Meshack Alloys** confirmed an acquisition process was underway, while the piece cited sources saying the company had run out of cash two months earlier after a year of cost cuts: a **July 2022** **10%** workforce trim, **54** layoffs and a wound-down supply line by **October 2022**, then **February 2023** exit from end-to-end fulfilment in **Nigeria** ([TechCrunch — Sendy shutdown][tc-sendy]).

The **rail** is **small and medium enterprise (SME)**-oriented **dispatch** and **fulfilment**—the unglamorous middle that connects factories to shop shelves when you lack **Amazon**-grade density. **Builders** are **Alloys**, **Evanson Biwott**, **Don Okoth**, and **Malaika Judd**, plus operations teams who lived inside **fuel** **inflation** and election-season **truck** **utilisation** swings. **Users** are retailers and brands outsourcing **stock** movement. **Referees** are **venture** **term sheets**, **asset** **financiers** (**Toyota** **Tsusho** appeared in earlier **TechCrunch** funding coverage linked from the shutdown story), and the **macro** **cycle**.

The article sketches how sharply sentiment turned: **Sendy** had reportedly been valued above **USD 80 million** late **2022** yet sought fresh capital at **USD 40–60 million** before a key investor backed out—leaving payroll and obligations uncovered ([TechCrunch — Sendy shutdown][tc-sendy]).

The tension is **scale economies versus burn**. **Logistics** **marketplaces** need volume *before* the **warehouse** rent comes due; **Africa**’s **B2B** **e-commerce** cohort learned that in public view during **2023**.

## Context

**Sendy** shared a **Silicon Savannah** funding window with [38. Twiga](38-twiga.md) and [41. MarketForce and Copia](41-marketforce-copia.md). **TechCrunch** explicitly grouped those struggles as a pattern—**operational** costs and thin **customer** pricing colliding with a **venture** slowdown ([TechCrunch — Sendy shutdown][tc-sendy]).

## History

### Rise: Toyota-era narrative

The shutdown piece links back to prior reporting on **Sendy**’s **USD 20 million** **Toyota**-backed raise (**2020**) and tallies **USD 26.5 million** in disclosed funding—context for how much **equity** fuel the model consumed before the **2023** cliff ([TechCrunch — Sendy shutdown][tc-sendy]).

### Fall: Nigeria retrenchment then terminal liquidity

**February 2023**’s **Nigeria** pullback was the last large geography trim before the **August** cessation narrative; **TechCrunch** noted talks with **Sabi**, **Wasoko**, **Trella**, and others on asset components ([TechCrunch — Sendy shutdown][tc-sendy]).

## Product and mechanics

### Composite scene: The half-empty return leg

*Composite, grounded in **economies-of-scale** logic central to the **TechCrunch** analysis:* A **truck** delivers **pallets** to **Kiambu** shops; the **dispatcher** struggles to backfill the return leg with paying freight—**margin** dies in **deadhead** kilometres.

Mechanically, **Sendy**-class operators need **load** **consolidation**, **cross-docking**, **proof-of-delivery** photos, and **working-capital** products that do not become unbooked **contingent** **liabilities**.

## Business model and incentives

Revenue from **shipper** fees competes with **capitalised** **fleet** and **tech** **debt**; **MOL PLUS** (**Mitsui O.S.K. Lines** corporate **venture**) appears in **TechCrunch** as the fraction of a hoped-for **USD 100 million** raise that actually closed—illustrating **strategic** versus **financial** investor mismatch ([TechCrunch — Sendy shutdown][tc-sendy]).

## Regulation and referees

**Cross-border** **customs** when **Nigeria** was live, **road** **safety** rules, and **labour** disputes on **rider** status shadow every **logistics** brand—cite local circulars when making legal claims. **Customer** and **driver** **personal** data require **Data Protection Act**, **2019** alignment ([Data Protection Act][dpa]).

## Adoption in Kenya

**Nairobi** industrial belts and **upcountry** distributor routes were natural first lanes; **Nigeria** expansion showed the temptation—and cost—of **multi-country** **burn**.

## Ecosystem effects

**Sendy**’s collapse red-pilled founders on **unit** **contribution** **margin** checks and **investor** **capital** **call** risk; **TechCrunch** used it to narrate a continent-wide **B2B** **commerce** reset ([TechCrunch — Sendy shutdown][tc-sendy]).

## Setbacks and controversies

Salary arrears, supplier **creditor** queues, and **acquisition** talks that may or may not close—report only what contemporaneous journalism can support ([TechCrunch — Sendy shutdown][tc-sendy]).

## Competition and alternatives

In-house **fleet** teams at **FMCGs**, **informal** **broker** networks, [40. Lori](40-lori-trucking.md)-style **long-haul** marketplaces, and **global** **forwarders**.

## Legacy and open questions

Legacy: **Kenya** proved **urban** **logistics** could attract **tier-one** **auto** and **shipping** capital—and still fail if **gross merchandise value (GMV)** **lagged** fixed cost. Open questions: **EV** **vans**, **rail** **intermodal** handoffs, and **SME** **credit** rails tied to **verified** **delivery** **events**.

## Builder read

*Interpretation.* Treat **venture** **signed** cheques as **zero** until **wired**. Build **route** **density** in one **city** before painting five flags on the pitch deck.
