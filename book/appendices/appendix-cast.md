# Appendix A: Cast

## How to use this appendix (quick key)

If you are new to Kenya's institutions, read each profile as a **navigation aid** rather than a definitive legal finding.

- **Role**: the person or collective function in the system.
- **Period**: the main era of relevance in this manuscript (approximate, for reader orientation).
- **Rail(s)**: the infrastructure lane most connected to that actor (connectivity, payments, APIs, state stack, education, logistics, etc.).
- **Key contribution**: the practical system effect attributed in this book.
- **Contested issues (neutral note)**: topics that have public disagreement, policy debate, or unresolved interpretation; listed in neutral language.
- **Where to find in book**: chapter links where this actor appears directly or contextually.

> **Editorial/legal note:** Entries flagged as contested or rights-sensitive should be reviewed in final legal edit before publication. This appendix is descriptive and non-adjudicative.

## Collective characters

### Agent network operators and agents (collective character)
- **Role:** Frontline cash-in/cash-out and trust intermediaries.
- **Period:** 2007 onward; central through all growth phases.
- **Rail(s):** Payments rail, distribution rail, merchant rail.
- **Key contribution:** Operationalized everyday reliability, liquidity handling, and customer trust for mobile money adoption.
- **Contested issues (neutral note):** Commission structures, fraud exposure, and labor precarity are discussed with mixed evidence and viewpoints.
- **Where to find in book:** [Part 3 — Money as a Network](part-03-money-as-a-network.md), [Part 6 — Merchant Rails](part-06-merchant-rails.md), [Part 11 — Credit Habit](part-11-credit-habit.md).

### Integrator engineers (collective character)
- **Role:** Technical bridge between APIs, legacy systems, and business workflows.
- **Period:** 2010s onward.
- **Rail(s):** API rail, banking integration rail, enterprise software rail.
- **Key contribution:** Reduced coordination failure between institutions by turning fragmented interfaces into deployable integrations.
- **Contested issues (neutral note):** Responsibility boundaries during outages, security events, or failed integrations are often diffuse.
- **Where to find in book:** [Part 5 — Banks Become Platforms](part-05-banks-become-platforms.md), [Part 9 — Global Apps, Local Physics](part-09-global-apps-local-physics.md), [Part 19 — Cybersecurity Era](part-19-cybersecurity-era.md).

### Regulators and oversight institutions (collective character: CA, CBK, ODPC, SASRA, parliamentary and judicial actors)
- **Role:** Rule-setting, supervision, dispute resolution, and rights-balancing authorities.
- **Period:** Continuous; especially visible from 2010 constitutional era onward.
- **Rail(s):** State stack rail, telecom governance rail, financial supervision rail, data-governance rail.
- **Key contribution:** Set guardrails and escalation channels for market conduct, consumer protection, and systemic stability.
- **Contested issues (neutral note):** Scope, sequencing, and proportionality of interventions are recurrent areas of policy disagreement.
- **Where to find in book:** [Part 7 — The State Stack](part-07-the-state-stack.md), [Part 11 — Credit Habit](part-11-credit-habit.md), [Part 13 — Community Finance](part-13-community-finance.md), [Part 19 — Cybersecurity Era](part-19-cybersecurity-era.md).

### SME operators and finance/admin staff (collective character)
- **Role:** Merchant adopters and operational decision-makers in small and medium businesses.
- **Period:** 2010s onward.
- **Rail(s):** Merchant payments rail, bookkeeping and reconciliation rail, working-capital rail.
- **Key contribution:** Revealed real-world viability gaps by testing platform promises against thin margins, cashflow volatility, and compliance burdens.
- **Contested issues (neutral note):** Debate persists on fee burden, platform dependency risk, and uneven bargaining power across merchant segments.
- **Where to find in book:** [Part 6 — Merchant Rails](part-06-merchant-rails.md), [Part 10 — Supply Chain Wars](part-10-supply-chain-wars.md), [Part 11 — Credit Habit](part-11-credit-habit.md), [Part 16 — Survival Tech](part-16-survival-tech.md).
