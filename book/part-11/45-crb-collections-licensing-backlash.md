# 45. Backlash: CRB Pain, Collections, Licensing

## Lead

The **notification** arrives at **2 a.m.**: a **contact** in your **phone book** gets a **text** about **your** **debt**. **Borrowers** **named** **lenders** in **parliamentary** **debates** and **social** **media** **threads**; **regulators** **answered** with **law**—not **moral** **sermons** alone. The **rail** **response** is **governed** **digital** **credit**: **Central Bank of Kenya (CBK)** **licensing**, **conduct** rules, **credit reference bureau (CRB)** **gatekeeping** for **unlicensed** **actors**, and **Data Protection Act, 2019** **leverage** against **abusive** **data** **use** ([TechCrunch — CBK licensing law][tc-law]; [DPA][dpa]; [DCP Regulations][dcp-regs]).

**TechCrunch**’s **December 2021** **pair** of **Kenya** **pieces** ties the **arc** together: **FinAccess** **2021** found **50.9%** of **respondents** **defaulting** on **digital** **app** **loans** and **flagged** **unfair** **collection** **practices** as a **driver** of **declining** **app** **usage**; **CBK** **barred** **unlicensed** **digital** **lenders** from **CRB** **reporting**, **dampening** **risk** **appetite** during the **licensing** **transition**; **parliament** then **empowered** **CBK** to **license** **digital** **credit** **providers** with **requirements** on **customer** **confidentiality** and **restrictions** on **sharing** **data** with **third-party** **debt** **collectors**—explicitly **framed** as **shielding** **borrowers** from **debt-shaming** ([TechCrunch — Fuliza vs apps][tc-fuliza]; [TechCrunch — CBK licensing law][tc-law]). **Builders** must **redesign** **collections** **stacks**; **users** **gain** **recourse** **language**; **referees** **inherit** **enforcement** **workloads**. The **shadow** is **tightening** **access** for **thin-file** **borrowers** if **compliance** **costs** **prune** **margins**—the **inclusion**–**protection** **trade-off** **this** **part** keeps **visible**.

## Context

Digital **credit** **boomed** on **M-PESA** **rails** ([42. Digital Lending Boom](42-digital-lending-boom.md)) **before** **product-specific** **supervision** **matured**. **Fuliza**’s **rise** ([44. Fuliza](44-fuliza.md)) **coincided** with **scrutiny** of **standalone** **apps**—**survey** **evidence** **suggested** **borrowers** **voted** with **feet** **and** **regulators** **closed** **loopholes** ([TechCrunch — Fuliza vs apps][tc-fuliza]).

## History

### **CRB** **drama** and **CBK** **directives**

**TechCrunch** **reported** **CBK** **blocking** **unlicensed** **digital** **lenders** from **submitting** **defaulter** **data** to **CRBs**—a **macro** **lever** that **changed** **underwriting** **incentives** **overnight** ([TechCrunch — Fuliza vs apps][tc-fuliza]).

### **2021** **statute** and **six-month** **window**

A **December 2021** **law** **signed** by the **president** **expanded** **CBK**’s **powers** to **license** and **supervise** **digital** **lenders**, with a **six-month** **application** **period** **TechCrunch** **emphasised** ([TechCrunch — CBK licensing law][tc-law]).

### **2022** **subsidiary** **rules** and **directory**

The [Digital Credit Providers Regulations, 2022][dcp-regs] **operationalise** **capital**, **reporting**, and **conduct** **expectations**; the [CBK directory][dcp-dir] **lists** **who** **passed** the **gate**.

## Product and mechanics

**Post-reform** **lending** **products** **must** **embed** **disclosure**, **consent** **artefacts**, **CRB** **logic** **that** **matches** **license** **status**, and **collections** **pathways** **that** **survive** **DPA** **review** ([DPA][dpa]; [ODPC][odpc]). **Apps** **that** **once** **externalised** **harassment** to **call-centre** **farms** **face** **API** **audits** and **contract** **clauses** **banning** **contact-book** **scraping** **abuse** **where** **rules** **bite** ([TechCrunch — CBK licensing law][tc-law]).

## Business model and incentives

**Revenue** **from** **penalties** and **rollovers** **shrinks** when **disclosure** **and** **fairness** **tests** **apply**; **licensed** **lenders** **shift** **toward** **repeat** **trust** **and** **lower** **churn** **rather** **than** **shock** **fees**. **Unlicensed** **operators** **lose** **CRB** **as** **a** **collection** **weapon**—**changing** **expected** **loss** **math** ([TechCrunch — Fuliza vs apps][tc-fuliza]).

## Regulation and referees

**CBK** (**DCP** **framework**), **Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC)** (**data** **rights**), **courts** (**private** **actions**), and **competition** **authority** **moves** on **periodic** **fee** **publication** **named** in **TechCrunch**’s **coverage** **form** a **mesh** ([TechCrunch — Fuliza vs apps][tc-fuliza]; [TechCrunch — CBK licensing law][tc-law]).

## Adoption in Kenya

**FinAccess** **2021** **metrics** **cited** by **TechCrunch**—**digital** **app** **usage** **down** **6.2** **points** to **2.1%**—read as **partial** **evidence** of **borrower** **retreat** **and** **regulatory** **chill** ([TechCrunch — Fuliza vs apps][tc-fuliza]; [FinAccess 2021][finaccess]).

## Ecosystem effects

**Formalisation** **pushed** **capital** **toward** **entities** **willing** **to** **run** **compliant** **ops**—**bank**–**telco** **products** and **large** **DCPs** **benefit** **relative** **to** **long-tail** **apps**. **Data** **governance** **consultancies** and **regtech** **vendors** **gained** **a** **Kenya** **anchor** **market**.

## Setbacks and controversies

**Debt-shaming**, **opaque** **pricing**, and **predatory** **APR** **claims** (**disputed** **by** **lenders**) **fuelled** **reform** **narratives** in **TechCrunch**’s **reporting** ([TechCrunch — CBK licensing law][tc-law]). **Implementation** **risk** **remains**: **rules** **on** **paper** **without** **enforcement** **cadence** **recreate** **old** **harms**.

## Competition and alternatives

**Informal** **lenders**, **savings** **groups**, **employer** **loans**, and **regulated** **digital** **products** **compete** for **the** **same** **stress** **moments**; **cross-border** **apps** **test** **enforcement** **perimeters**.

## Legacy and open questions

Will **CRB** **data** **quality** **improve** **enough** **to** **lower** **prices** **for** **good** **borrowers**, or **will** **exclusion** **persist** **as** **a** **blunt** **instrument**? **Open** **banking**-style **data** **sharing** **may** **recast** **consent** **battles** **the** **2019** **Act** **only** **began**.

## Builder read

*Interpretation.* **Treat** **collections** **as** **a** **regulated** **channel** **like** **KYC**: **logged**, **reviewed**, and **aligned** **with** **DPA** **purpose** **limits**. **Map** **every** **third-party** **processor**; **revocation** **of** **DCP** **licence** **is** **existential** **risk** **now** **on** **the** **table** ([TechCrunch — CBK licensing law][tc-law]).
