# 44. Fuliza: Ambient Borrowing

## Lead

The **till** **beeps**, the **merchant** waits, and the payer does not open a **loan app**—they tap through a **prompt** and **M-PESA** completes anyway. **Fuliza** is **unsecured** **overdraft** on **Kenya**’s dominant **mobile money** wallet: **Safaricom** customers **draw** **short** **liquidity** at the moment **balance** would otherwise fail ([Safaricom — Fuliza demystified][sf-demystify]). The **rail** is **embedded** **credit** inside **pay**, **send**, and **buy** flows—partners **KCB** **Bank** and **NCBA** **Bank** sit on the **licensed** **banking** side of the product **TechCrunch** described as formally registered while **app** lenders scrambled for **CBK** **approvals** ([TechCrunch — Fuliza vs apps][tc-fuliza]). **Builders** are **telco** **product** **teams**, **bank** **risk** **committees**, and **prudential** **supervisors**. **Users** are everyday **M-PESA** **customers** smoothing **hours** or **days**. **Referees** are **CBK** and **competition** **policy** on **transparency**—including periodic **fee** **disclosure** expectations **TechCrunch** noted alongside **digital** **lender** **reforms** ([TechCrunch — Fuliza vs apps][tc-fuliza]).

**FinAccess 2021**—via **TechCrunch**’s **summary**—put **national** **Fuliza** **uptake** at **18.3%** while **digital** **loan** **app** usage fell to **2.1%**, attributing part of the swing to **competition** from **formal** **digital** **credit** and **collection** **practices** on apps ([TechCrunch — Fuliza vs apps][tc-fuliza]; [FinAccess 2021][finaccess]). **Safaricom**’s **2020/21** **sustainability** **report**, **quoted** by **TechCrunch**, placed **Fuliza** **disbursements** around **USD 3.1 billion** for the year—roughly **USD 12 million** a day against **23.8 million** **M-PESA** **customers** in that **narrative** ([TechCrunch — Fuliza vs apps][tc-fuliza]; [Safaricom 2021 Sustainable Business Report][sf-sust21]). The **shadow** is **invisibility**: **debt** **fractured** across **micro** **transactions** **accumulates** **without** the **psychological** **weight** of a **single** **loan** **dashboard**—until **repayment** **pressure** **arrives** **anyway**.

## Context

**Fuliza** stacks on [Part III](../part-03/index.md) **M-PESA** **ubiquity** and follows **M-Shwari**’s proof that **credit** can live **inside** the **SIM** **menu**. It competes directly with [43. Tala and Branch](43-tala-branch.md) for **same-day** **liquidity** but wins **friction** because **no** **separate** **app** **install** is required at the **moment** of **failure** ([TechCrunch — Fuliza vs apps][tc-fuliza]).

## History

**TechCrunch** (**December 2021**) framed **Fuliza** as roughly **three** **years** post-launch—i.e. a **2018**-era **introduction** in **market** **memory**—already **out-competing** **Silicon** **Valley**-linked **apps** on **survey** **uptake** ([TechCrunch — Fuliza vs apps][tc-fuliza]). **Regulatory** **tightening** on **unlicensed** **CRB** **reporting** **tilted** **risk** **appetite** on **apps** while **Fuliza** **stayed** inside **bank**–**telco** **guardrails** **observers** **described** as **formal** ([TechCrunch — Fuliza vs apps][tc-fuliza]).

## Product and mechanics

Per **Safaricom**’s **operator** **copy**, **Fuliza** covers **M-PESA** **transactions** when **funds** are **short**, subject to **limits** and **fees**, with **recovery** on **incoming** **funds** ([Safaricom — Fuliza demystified][sf-demystify]). The **UX** is **continuity**—the **payment** **completes**—rather than **interruption** **plus** **redirect** to a **credit** **funnel**.

## Business model and incentives

**Fee** **income** **shares** among **telco** and **bank** **partners**; **TechCrunch**’s **macro** **figures** **imply** **enormous** **velocity**—**billions** in **annual** **flows**—driven by **small** **tickets** **repeated** **often** ([TechCrunch — Fuliza vs apps][tc-fuliza]). **Strategic** **incentive** for **Safaricom** is **retention** of **M-PESA** **as** **default** **rail**; for **banks**, **fee** **yield** and **depositor** **touchpoints**.

## Regulation and referees

**Fuliza** **operates** as a **licensed** **bank**–**telco** **construct** in **commentary** **contrasted** with **grey-market** **apps** ([TechCrunch — Fuliza vs apps][tc-fuliza]). **CBK**-led **financial** **inclusion** **surveys** and **competition** **authority** **moves** on **fee** **disclosure** appear in the same **journalistic** **bundle** as **digital** **lender** **licensing** ([TechCrunch — Fuliza vs apps][tc-fuliza]).

## Adoption in Kenya

**18.3%** **Fuliza** **uptake** vs **2.1%** **digital** **app** **usage** (**FinAccess** **2021** **via** **TechCrunch**) is the **headline** **comparator** for how **embedded** **credit** **outran** **standalone** **apps** in that **window** ([TechCrunch — Fuliza vs apps][tc-fuliza]; [FinAccess 2021][finaccess]).

## Ecosystem effects

**Ambient** **borrowing** **trained** **users** to **treat** **wallet** **balance** as **elastic**—reshaping **personal** **cash** **management** and **merchant** **expectations** that **payments** **clear** **first** **time**. It also **raised** the **bar** for **loan** **apps**: **compete** on **price** and **trust**, not just **speed** ([TechCrunch — Fuliza vs apps][tc-fuliza]).

## Setbacks and controversies

**FinAccess** **respondents** still **listed** **mobile** **banking** **loans** “**including** **Fuliza**” among **top** **default** **categories**—**TechCrunch** **noted** the **blended** **bucket**—so **embedded** **credit** is **not** **immune** to **stress** ([TechCrunch — Fuliza vs apps][tc-fuliza]). **Transparency** and **cost** **awareness** remain **consumer** **pain** points in **survey** **commentary** relayed by **press** ([TechCrunch — Fuliza vs apps][tc-fuliza]).

## Competition and alternatives

**Bank** **overdrafts**, **credit** **cards** (thin **penetration**), **chamas**, **digital** **DCP** **apps**, and **salary** **timing** **adjustments** are **substitutes**; **Fuliza**’s **edge** is **contextual** **availability** at **checkout**.

## Legacy and open questions

Will **open** **finance** and **multi** **wallet** **interoperability** **fragment** **Fuliza**-style **dominance** or **cement** **incumbent** **distribution**? How will **regulators** **measure** **indebtedness** when **credit** is **invisible** **across** **dozens** of **tiny** **draws**?

## Builder read

*Interpretation.* **Embedded** **finance** **wins** on **latency** and **trust** **inheritance** from the **host** **rail**. **Product** **managers** should **model** **cumulative** **cost** **surfaces**—not **per** **txn** **quotes**—because **political** and **survey** **risk** **tracks** **totals**, **not** **moments**.
